About Pete

Four decades, one obsession.

Born and raised in small-town Indiana, Pete has been racing, fixing, and building since boyhood. A certified toolmaker and graduate engineer with 40+ years in the stamping industry alongside automotive manufacturers.

His 3-bay garage is now home to high-end restoration and ground-up assembly — and to anyone who wants to learn the craft hands-on.
