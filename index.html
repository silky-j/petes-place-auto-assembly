Pete's Garage History

It started at thirteen.

Pete's first car was a 1970½ Camaro he bought at 13 — pulled the engine, rebuilt it, and "sold it before I knew what I was doing." Five decades of builds later, here's the road that got him here.

About Pete

Four decades, one obsession.

Born and raised in small-town Indiana, Pete was racing by 10 and owned three cars by 16. A certified toolmaker and graduate engineer, he spent 40+ years in the stamping industry working alongside automotive manufacturers.

After reading about kit cars in 1983, he set out to build one himself — and never stopped. His three-bay garage is now home to high-end restoration and ground-up assembly, and to anyone who wants to learn the craft hands-on.
